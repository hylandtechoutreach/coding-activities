Heart-Healthy Foods

Heart Healthy Foods

Pick three of your favorite foods from the list below, and add them as <li></li> elements to the <ul></ul> in HTML!

It should look something like this, with YOUR_ITEMs replaced by the foods:

<ul>
    <li>
        YOUR_ITEM1
    </li>
    <li>
        YOUR_ITEM2
    </li>
    <li>
        YOUR_ITEM3
    </li>
</ul>

List

  • Salmon
  • Tuna
  • Almonds
  • Walnuts
  • Blueberries
  • Strawberries
  • Beans
  • Tofu
  • Carrots
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Tomatoes
  • Spinach
  • Broccoli
  • Oranges
  • Dark Chocolate

Source: https://health.clevelandclinic.org/12-heart-healthy-foods-to-work-into-your-diet/
